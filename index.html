LEEDS

Delivery direct to your door, every Tuesday – Saturday

Crosstown is a London brand specialising in award-winning doughnuts, handcrafted cookies, and small-batch ice cream. We roll our signature sourdough dough by hand in our London barkery, with all of our jams, compotes, fillings, glazes, and toppings made in-house, using the finest seasonal ingredients. Our mission is to satisfy your tastebuds every day, all year round. For the very first time in 2020, we began delivering our premium sweet treats and curated gifts all over the country, including the city of Leeds – so you’re never too far from your Crosstown fix.

WHAT LEEDS OFFERS

The city of Leeds

Home to over 200,000 students, Leeds is one of England’s most rapidly expanding cities. The city is bursting with energy and restaurant and café growth has continued on an upwards trajectory since 2013. Not only is Leeds the home of the fizzy drink, but it’s also where the original Marks & Spencer shop was created in its famous Kirkgate Market.

FAQS

  • How do i place an order?

    Order online at www.crosstown.co.uk/order-now and select England, Wales, & lowland Scotland. Follow the instructions to place an order for future deliveries.

  • Do you offer vegan doughnuts, cookies, and gifts?

    We do! We’ve got an award-winning vegan range, which is available for next day delivery across Leeds and the rest of the UK./p>

  • Where can i view your menu/prices?

    Head to the nationwide menu to view our selection of doughnuts, cookies, merchandise, and gifts. To place an order head to crosstown nationwide order and follow the instructions.

  • How fast is delivery in Leeds?

    Order by midday for next day delivery across Leeds and the rest England, Wales & lowland Scotland. We delivery nationwide Tuesday – Saturday every week.

  • Do you have a store in Leeds?

    We don’t have a store in Leeds yet. However, you can place an online order via the Crosstown website for a next day doughnut delivery direct to your doorstep, Tuesday – Saturday every week.
